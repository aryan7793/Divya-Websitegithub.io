  • Equipment Used: Computer, Google Docs, Internet Access, Surveys and interviews.
  • Drive URL: Project Link

Abstract
An international culture of Kpop continues, powered by rich music in addition to electrifying performances. This research will investigate the event management strategies possessed in this industry that is beneficial to the popularity of Kpop music shows. From the Kim Sisters in the 50s, Kpop has come a long way through four generations with more than 300 bands active as of date. From small local performances to large international shows, event management has been a vital aspect in displaying Kpop talent. The findings of this research are based on the data collected from different sites and the fans who follow Kpop and are engaged in it.
